About the game

Project Flight Game

This project is part of the software 2 course. The website has been produced according to Ilkka's instructions on YouTube.
After the starter code, we have edited and added things to fit our project.

How to play?

The purpose of the game is to achieve as many goals as possible withing the CO2 budget.
There are eight different goals:

  • Shorty Shorts | Temperature over +25°C
  • T-Shirt | Temperature between +20°C to +25°C
  • Jeans | Temperature between +15°C to +20°C
  • Jacket | Temperature between +5°C to +15°C
  • Lovely Slush | Temperature between +0°C to +5°C
  • Long Johns | Temperature under -15°C
  • Cloudy | Clouds
  • Windy | Wind blows more than 10 m/s

Game starts from Helsinki-Vantaa Airport. Current airport is shown as a green dot on the map.
Available airports are shown as blue dots. Choose your path carefully - CO2 is not unlimited!
